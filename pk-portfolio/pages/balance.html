Balance is an app that, like a checkbook, allows you to keep track of your life. Enter your activities and rewards to help balance your life. The app helps users choose what kind of lifestyle they want to live.

Balance App

The Balance app gives you a sleek, fast way of balancing your daily activities, similar to balancing a checkbook. The intuitive design and customization allows users to quickly make the app fit their plan.

Set your goals. You already know what you want to do and what you should do in life, Balance helps keep you on track. Maybe youâ€™ve been working too hard and need to take more personal time. Any situation can be applied in Balance.

Balance keeps a log of your activities and rewards. All transactions are detailed by activity type, date, and its effect on your balance.

Watch your progress as you enter activities. Every second counts, whether its a pick-up sports team, taking the stairs, or an afterwork run. Use some of those hard earned points to treat yourself to a drink or dine out with some friends. Increase the difficulty by making your healthy activites worth less and your not so healthy activites cost more. Its not just about fitness though, create any relationship you want.
